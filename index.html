About

Matthew Renze

AI researcher, consultant, and author

Over the past two decades, I've taught over 500,000 software developers and IT professionals how to leverage the power of AI in responsible and ethical ways. My clients range from government agencies, to Fortune 500 companies, to small software startups around the globe.

I'm the owner of Renze Consulting, a for-profit AI consulting firm.
I'm also the president of Serenze Global a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicating to improving access to technology education. And, I'm a Microsoft MVP in AI, a Microsoft Regional Director, an ASPInsider, and an author for Pluralsight, Udemy, and SkillShare.
